About Me

My name is Chadd Artaud and I was born and raised in San Diego, California. I am a 23 year old who is beginning his journey into software engineering. My first experience with coding was in my undergraduate at the University of Nevada, Reno where I earned my Bachelors of Science in Mechanical Engineering. I enjoy solving challenging problems as there is no better feeling than finally figuring a problem that you have been working on for a long period of time.


When I first started learning software development, I was a self learner for about 6 months. Once I had a firm grasp of the basics, I decided to take the next step and sign up for a full stack software development bootcamp through University of San Diego Extension program. My goal for myself is to be working remotely as a software engineer by the end of this year where I can continuously learn and improve my software engineering skills!
