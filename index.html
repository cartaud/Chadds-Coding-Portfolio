About Me

My name is Chadd Artaud and I was born and raised in San Diego, California. I am a 23 year old who is beginning his journey into software engineering. My first experience with coding was in my undergraduate at the University of Nevada, Reno where I earned my Bachelors of Science in Mechanical Engineering.


Graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Nevada Reno and successfully passed the Fundamentals of Engineering (FE) and Certified SolidWorks Associate (CSWA) exams. I have led multiple projects, including as a Mechanical Engineering intern designing a calibration system for nuclear instrumentation.
